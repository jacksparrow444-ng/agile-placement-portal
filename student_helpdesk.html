Placement Process Guidelines

1. Ensure your profile is updated and CGPA is verified.
2. Keep an eye on the Job Feed for upcoming drives.
3. Apply carefully, as you cannot withdraw after shortlisting.
4. Check 'Notifications' tab for interview schedules and offer letters.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who can view my resume?

Once you apply for a specific job, only that company's HR and the TPO office can view your resume.

Q: Can I update my profile after applying?

Yes, your profile updates will instantly reflect for all future and pending applications automatically.

Q: Whom should I contact for portal issues?

You can use the Inquiry Form on this page to directly contact the Placement Cell Administrator.
